Voice cloning lifecycle

A clone is just a voice_id. Once you have one, it slots into the regular speech endpoint exactly where a catalogue voice would.

Step 1 Upload sample 10-30 sec WAV, single speaker, plus consent JSON in a multipart/form-data POST. POST /v1/voices
returns id
Step 2 Get a voice_id back Persisted on your account. Reusable across as many synthesis calls as you want. { "id": "voice_abc123…" }
use as voice_id
Step 3 Synthesize as normal Same endpoint as a default voice, same body shape, same streaming + speech marks support. POST /v1/audio/speech
Cleanup is your call. One-off jobs delete the voice after use (DELETE /v1/voices/{id}). Long-running narrators (podcast host, recurring character) keep the voice_id and reuse it indefinitely. No re-clone needed.
